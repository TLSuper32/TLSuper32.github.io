Y2 Desktop App

Welcome to the YouTube Uploader API Desktop App product page. This app allows you to upload videos / view analytics to your YouTube channel with ease. Just authenticate with your Google account and the app will take care of the rest!

Main Features:

  • Easy authentication with your Google/YouTube account.
  • Upload videos directly from your desktop to your YouTube channel.
  • Set video privacy settings, categories, and tags during the upload process.
  • Monitor the status of your video uploads in real time.
  • Automatically retry failed uploads.

Permissions & Privacy:

This app requires access to your Google/YouTube account for the following purposes:

  • Access your YouTube channel to upload videos on your behalf.
  • Retrieve your YouTube channel details to display in the app.
